The Spenzer is recognized as a Top 10 hotel in New York City by Travel + Leisure's 2021 World's Best Awards.

       Since its debut on October 1st, 1907, The Spenzer Hotel has remained a New York icon hosting world leaders, dignitaries, captains of industry, Broadway legends and Hollywood royalty. As an established staple for lavish society affairs, and blockbuster films, The Spenzer has welcomed guests from around the world to enjoy its magic at the castle on Central Park South for more than 100 years.

Location

       Located on the corner of 5th Avenue and Central Park South, The Spenzer Hotel is at the center of it all. Steps from Bergdorf Goodman, Barneys, and an array of the most famous shopping destinations in the world, The Spenzer’s address is as convenient as it is luxurious. Other nearby attractions include: Columbus Circle ,Central Park, Central Park Zoo, Carnegie Hall, Radio City Music Hall, St. Patrick’s Cathedral, Rockefeller Center, Museum of Modern Art, and Lincoln Center.

Accommodations

       Offering 282 distinctive guestrooms including 102 luxurious suites, The Spenzer continues to strike the balance between its storied past and limitless future with the launch of the Legacy Suite collection. Classic crown molding is juxtaposed against contemporary works of art and lavish fabrics, adding vibrant textural elements to The Spenzer's guestrooms complete with dramatic lighting and beautifully finished woodwork to amplify the impeccable motif.

Sustainable Responsibility

*The Spenzer encourages reuse of towels, sheets, and other linens to reduce wasteful water practices.
*Our rooftop is home to a bee sanctuary & herb garden, producing ingredients for our dining outlets.
*All lighting in The Spenzer is provided by energy efficient, LED bulbs.
