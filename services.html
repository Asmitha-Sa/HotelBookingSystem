Spenzer Services And Specialities

         Spenzer offers exclusive services and benefits for the comfort of its guests. Among many facilities that the hotel has to offer, guests can directly access to the   shopping mall and the theme & amusement park by car park hotel entrance elevators. A various choice of restaurants and cafés are available at the shopping   mall. The hotel's family-friendly and home-like accommodation concept also provide many advantages such as a supermarket, a pharmacy , a hair dresser and   many more within a walking distance.


     Food:   The hotel restaurant with both indoor and outdoor seating offers an open-buffet and is directly accessible from guestroom corridors.

     Room Service:   Spenzer provides 24-hour room service. Please click here for Room Service menu.

     Parking:   Spenzer offers 24-hour indoor parking for its guests. The hotel is directly accessible from the indoor car park and guests can easily reach the shopping mall by using the elevators   that are facing the car park hotel entrance. The indoor parking and valet services are free for accommodating hotel guests. Car washing service is also available with an additional cost.

     Business Centre:   Spenzer provides significant services for a flawless business-travel experience. The hotel's business centre is designated to meet all your business travel needs and   available to assist you with all office services such as scanning and emailing documents, fax services, photocopying, printing and offices supplies.

     Free Shuttle Service:   The accommodating hotel guests can benefit of free shuttle service provided by Spenzer Shopping & Entertainment World based on availability.

     Internet Access:   Spenzer offers free Internet access. High-speed wireless Internet connection is complimentary both in the rooms and public areas.



   Other Facilities
  • Rent-a-car
  • Airline reservation and confirmation
  • Babysitter upon request
  • Multilingual staff
  • 24-hour Manager on Duty
  • Dry cleaning and laundry
  • Major credit cards accepted



   Immediate Vicinity

     Supermarket:   One of the biggest chains of supermarkets in Turkey, Migros Supermarket is located in Spenzer shopping mall. In addition to basic groceries, Migros Supermarket offers a   large spectrum of products such as stationary, glassware and many other non-food items.

     Banks and ATM Machines:   There are two banks and ATM machines inside Spenzer Shopping Mall. You can easily perform all your digital and mobile banking transactions at the   banks. The ATM machines provide 24/7 service.

     Pharmacy:   Located inside Spenzer Shopping Mall, the pharmacy sells cosmetics and skincare products in addition to medical and healthcare products.

     Hair Dresser:   Located in Spenzer Shopping Mall, the unisex hairdresser serves both men and women.
